Our Dental Services

Comprehensive care tailored to your needs using modern techniques.

General & Preventive

Regular check-ups, dental cleanings (scaling & polishing), fluoride application, and oral-health consultations.

Restorative

Dental fillings, high-quality crowns, bridges, inlays, and onlays to restore damaged or decayed teeth.

Prosthodontics

Complete dentures, partial dentures (removable prosthetics), fixed partial dentures, and artificial-tooth replacements.

Extractions

Child Friendly

Conservative, minimally invasive treatment plans designed specifically to ensure comfort and safety for kids.

About Geetha Dental Clinic

Geetha Dental Clinic is a trusted general dental centre located in the Kammagondanahalli–Jalahalli West (Abbigere) area of North Bengaluru. We have been serving the local community for about 22 years, offering pain-minimal, patient-friendly dental care.

With modern equipment and hygienic protocols, the clinic helps patients of all ages maintain healthy, confident smiles.


Why Choose Us?

  • 22+ years of long-standing practice in North Bengaluru
  • Focus on preventive care and long-term oral health
  • Well-equipped clinic with strict sterilization protocols
  • Child- and senior-friendly, welcoming environment
  • Conveniently located near Wimac Circle with easy parking

Primary Dentist

Dr. Priya Harsha (BDS)

28+ Years of Clinical Experience

Dr. Priya Harsha completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery from Maruthi Dental College in 1996. Since then, she has been consistently practicing in Bangalore, leading a friendly, knowledge-oriented team committed to making each patient's visit calm and informative.

She emphasizes gentle, patient-centered treatment and is known for clear communication, tailoring plans specifically to the needs of children, adults, and seniors.
